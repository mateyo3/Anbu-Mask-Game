The Anbu Mask Game

There are four Anbu masks. By clicking on a mask you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to 's number, you lose the game if your total score goes above 's number. The value of each Anbu mask is hidden from you until you click on it. When the game restarts, the game will change the values of each Anbu mask.
